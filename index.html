Installation:

Create an empty folder named as the module name in your
[proxy folder]/bin/node_modules, then download the module.json file into it.

Dev Name Download Description
Mount-Prediction [click] Removes ping tax related to mounts such as `skill -> mount` and `unmount -> skill` Also acts as a way to change your mount client side
Ghillieglade Reset [click] Automatically resets all dungeons whenever you enter Velik's Sanctuary so you don't have to /reset manually.
Tuwanguide [click] Calls out the colors for the Tuwangi minigame/guardian mission in proxy chat to make things a lot easier.
No More Motes [click] Hides HP/MP recovery motes to improve your FPS when mass farming IoD.
Salt Remover [click] Simple mod which can remove annoying messages
Achievements [click] Tracks achievement progress and makes it fairly easy to check if you have specific achievements and which of its requirements are already done.
Antaroth's Abyss Guide [click] Calls out attacks during the last bossfight in AANM/AAHM.
Generic Box Opener / Item User [click] Mass-opens boxes and mass-uses items.
Block List [click] Makes your blocked player list global across all characters on a server.
Auto-Camera [click] Increases your maximum camera distance beyond any limitations
Auto-Cutscene [click] Automatically cancels movies/cutscenes.
Enrage Notifier [click] Notifies you about boss enrage events.
Auto-Vanguard [click] Automatically turns in vanguard quests as soon as they're completed.
Teabagger [click] A tera-proxy module that lets you teabag people.
AFKer [click] Prevents you from being logged out to the character select screen when AFK.
Player Inspector [click] Automatically inspects players when they apply for your group and logs details about them in your proxy console and ingame chat.
Vanguardian [click] Automatically finishes Vanguard Initiative quests on completion.
Cosplayer [click] A tera-proxy module able to change your client-side appearance. Changes to your character are saved on your hard drive and reloaded on next login.
Essentials [click] Automatically uses the Everful Nostrum and Complete Crystal Bind whenever needed.
